Instagram Non-Followers Tracker

Follow these steps to find users who don't follow you back on Instagram:

1. Log into Instagram: Open Instagram (website, in browser), and log into your account.
2. Visit Instagram Data Download: Request your data download on this page.
3. Select "followers and following" under "connections": Choose "followers and following" in the "connections" section. Opt for "JSON" format, and select all-time data for "Date Range".
4. Check your email: You'll receive an email containing a .zip file.
5. Extract and locate files: Extract the zip file, find "following.json" and "followers_1.json".
